Two-Toed Sloth

This is a baby two-toed sloth. Sloths eat buds, tender shoots, and leaves, mainly of cecropia and guarumo trees. Sloths curved claws, measuring up to 10 cm (almost 4 inches) long, are shaped to hook around trees.
  • Scientific Name: Choloepus
  • Average Weight: 6 kg (13 lb)
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years/wild. 30-40 years/captivity.
  • Habitat: Treetops of rainforests and deciduous forests.
